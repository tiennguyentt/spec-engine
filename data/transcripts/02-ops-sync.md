# FlowBook — Ops sync (PM, Ops Lead)

**Attendees:** Tien (PM), Linh (Ops Lead)
**Topic:** How salons actually operate today, before we freeze the v1 spec

---

**Linh (Ops Lead):** One thing about deposits: in practice our pilot salons skip
deposits for walk-ins and for regulars they trust. If we force a deposit on a
regular standing in the shop, the owner will just bypass the app.

**Tien (PM):** The CTO said every booking through the app takes a deposit, no
exceptions.

**Linh (Ops Lead):** I'm telling you what happens on the floor. At least flag it.

**Tien (PM):** Noted. What about cancellations?

**Linh (Ops Lead):** Officially it's 24 hours, but most owners quietly honor
cancellations up to 48 hours before, they'd rather keep the customer relationship
than the deposit.

**Tien (PM):** OK, that's another gap against the announced policy. Anything on
reminders?

**Linh (Ops Lead):** Reminders work. One customer story: a lady got the reminder
while abroad and couldn't cancel because the app needed an OTP via SMS to her
local number. Probably an edge case, but support spent an hour on it.

**Tien (PM):** Good catch. Service durations?

**Linh (Ops Lead):** Salons want per-staff durations eventually, the same haircut
takes the senior stylist 30 minutes and the junior 50. For v1 per-service is fine.
